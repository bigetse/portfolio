After earning my HCI degree from Brunel University, I explored a different career for 4 years as a FMCG Account Executive and Manager at a marketing agency.

In 2019, I returned to tech and chose design because it is closest to human problems. I am fortunate to have worked with leaders who trusted me with big responsibilites early in my design career leading to rapid growth. I’ve managed teams, led projects and shaped processes while contributing to impactful products at startups and large organisations.

My strengths lie in strategy and planning and I enjoy the discovery, conceptualisation and ideation stages of design best. At work, I'm known for big-picture thinking and thoroughness (don't hold me to that if there are a few errors in my portfolio).
